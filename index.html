Learning Journeys

We went to 3 learning journeys in Term 2 Week 9: Natural History Museum for Science; Chinatown for History; MacRitchie Reservoir for Geography. It was a fruitful expereince and I learnt a lot from the trips.

Science:

We went to Natural History Museum and learned classification of living organisms to start off with Biology in Term 3. Furthermore, we had the opportunity to try and identify the organism samples by using the classification methods we had learnt earlier on.

History:

We went to Chinatown and studied the life of the early Chinese migrants that came to Singapore. We had to write an Autobiography in the perspective of an Chinese migrant. However, some of the other groups went to Kampong Glam and some went to Little India.

Geography:

We went to MacRitchie Reservoir and collected some water samples for testing. We tested the turbidity, amount of dissolved oxygen, pH and analysed the data to conclude the overall water quality.
